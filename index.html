Our Story

Why We're Building This

We downloaded seven budgeting apps together.

We quit all seven.

They were built for individuals. We're a couple. One of us is a budget nerd who wants to track every dollar. The other just wants to know if we can afford that weekend trip.

Every app forced us into the same mold: either we both obsess over budgeting, or neither of us does.

So we started researching. We interviewed hundreds of couples. We launched a podcast about money and relationships. And we discovered something important:

The apps weren't failing because couples are bad at budgeting. The apps were failing because they were never designed for couples in the first place.

So we're building Planzo. Not for everyone. For couples who:

  • Want to manage money together without losing independence
  • Have different approaches to budgeting (nerd + free spirit)
  • Are tired of fighting about $6 coffees
  • Want a system that works for how they actually live

We're building this in public. Sharing our progress. Learning from couples like you. And creating something that doesn't exist yet: a budgeting app that actually gets how relationships work.
